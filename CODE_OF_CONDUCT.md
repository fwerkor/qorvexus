# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Qorvexus to be a constructive, technically serious, and respectful project.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and direct
- assume good intent while giving clear technical feedback
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and decisions rather than people
- avoid harassment, intimidation, or hostile personal attacks
- respect privacy and security boundaries

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or abusive language
- discriminatory remarks
- doxxing or threats
- deliberately disruptive behavior
- sharing private data, credentials, or security-sensitive material without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, or pull requests that violate this code of conduct.

For serious concerns, contact:

- `admin@fwerkor.com`

## Maintainer Authority

The FWERKOR Team is responsible for interpreting and enforcing this policy.
